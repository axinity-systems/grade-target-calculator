# Grade Target Calculator

A student-focused academic planning tool developed by Axinity Labs.

Grade Target Calculator helps students understand their current academic standing, evaluate future possibilities, and determine the performance required to achieve their goals.

Rather than acting as a simple percentage calculator, the platform is designed as an academic planning system that answers questions such as:

* What is my current percentage?
* What do I need to score to achieve my target?
* What will my final result look like if I continue performing at a certain level?
* What is the highest score I can still achieve?
* What GPA do I need to reach my academic goals?

The calculator relies entirely on transparent and deterministic mathematics. No AI predictions, percentile estimations, rank forecasts, or external APIs are used. Every result is generated using formulas that can be independently verified.

## Features

### Target Calculator

Determine the marks and percentage required in remaining assessments to achieve a desired final score.

### Forecast Calculator

Predict final academic outcomes based on expected future performance.

### Potential Calculator

Explore best-case, worst-case, and trend-based scenarios to understand the range of possible outcomes.

### GPA Planner

Calculate the GPA required across remaining credits to achieve a target cumulative GPA.

## Project Goals

The objective of Grade Target Calculator is to help students make informed academic decisions using clear, understandable mathematics instead of speculation.

Built with the belief that students should always know:

1. Where they stand today.
2. What is realistically achievable.
3. What actions are required to reach their goals.
